# Interview Questions for Abiodun Alade

## Introduction

Abiodun Alade is the Communications Specialist at Dangote Industries Limited, responsible for managing public relations and stakeholder communications. As part of the investigative story on the Dangote Refinery's governance and environmental challenges, these questions aim to explore how the company communicates its HSE policies, engages with communities, and addresses criticisms related to environmental and social impacts.

## Context

The Dangote Refinery, Africa's largest (by doubling its capcity, it'd become the world largest), is a cornerstone of Nigeria's energy transition, but it faces scrutiny over environmental impacts and social disruptions. Your responses will provide insights into the company's communication strategies, transparency measures, and efforts to build trust with stakeholders, helping to understand how narratives around pollution, displacement, and unfulfilled promises are managed.

Questions focus on communication strategies, transparency, stakeholder engagement, and handling of environmental and social narratives, aligned with the themes of HSE, sustainability, environmental pollution, displacement, and unfulfilled promises.

### Communication Strategies and Transparency

- How does Dangote Industries Limited communicate its HSE policies and sustainability initiatives to the public and stakeholders, particularly regarding environmental impacts like gas flaring and emissions?
- What measures are in place to ensure transparency in reporting environmental data, such as air and water quality around the refinery, and how are these communicated to affected communities?
- How does the company handle media inquiries and public criticisms related to pollution, displacement, and unfulfilled promises from past agreements like the 2007 MOU?

### Stakeholder Engagement and Community Relations

- Can you describe Dangote's approach to engaging with local communities and stakeholders in areas impacted by the refinery, including efforts to address concerns about forced evictions and livelihood disruptions?
- What role does the communications team play in the company's expansion plans, such as the 50% capacity increase, and how are these communicated to balance economic benefits with environmental stewardship?
- How does Dangote address narratives around its contribution to Nigeria's climate goals, including pledges to reduce GHG emissions, and what challenges arise in communicating these efforts effectively?
- How does Dangote address perceptions of corruption in land acquisitions or IFC-funded projects, and what transparency measures are in place?

These questions are open-ended for detailed responses, drawing from research papers and news stories for credibility.
